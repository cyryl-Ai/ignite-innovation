Guidelines

Welcome to the Ignite Innovation competition guidelines page. Here, we provide you with comprehensive information to ensure a successful participation experience.

Submission Guidelines

  • Prepare a detailed project proposal outlining your innovative idea.
  • Include visual aids, such as images, diagrams, or videos, to enhance your proposal.
  • Submit your proposal in PDF format through our online submission platform.

Evaluation Criteria

  • Creativity and Originality: Your idea should showcase innovative thinking.
  • Feasibility: Demonstrate the practicality and viability of your concept.
  • Impact: Highlight the potential positive impact of your innovation.

For more detailed guidelines and any clarifications, feel free to contact us.
